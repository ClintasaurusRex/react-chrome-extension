About TLDR Extension

The Content Summarizer Extension is a powerful tool designed to help users quickly understand and manage web content. With this extension, you can easily summarize selected text, store and manage your summaries, and access a variety of additional features to enhance your browsing experience.

Features

  • Text Summarization: Quickly summarize selected text to grasp the main points without reading the entire content.
  • Copy summaries to clipboard
  • And more...
