T.L.D.R.

Too Long; Didn't Read browser extension is a powerful tool designed to help users quickly understand and manage web content. With this extension, you can easily summarize selected text, store and manage your summaries, and access a variety of additional features to enhance your browsing experience.

Features

  • Text Summarization: Quickly summarize selected text to grasp the main points without reading the entire content.
  • Summarize Entire Web Pages: Instantly summarize the entire content of any web page.
  • Chat with GPT-4o: Engage in dynamic conversations with GPT-4, send prompts, and get responses right from the extension.
  • Copy Summaries to Clipboard: Easily copy the generated summaries to your clipboard for quick sharing.
  • Download Summaries to your device: Easily download the generated summaries to your device in txt format.
  • And more...
